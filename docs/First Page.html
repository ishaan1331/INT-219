Top 10 Adventure Sports With Places

Ever wanted to take a break from your life and go on an adventurous trip and just forget what else is going on ? If your answer is yes , than this article is definitely for you . We have listed the ten best adventure sports for the hidden adventurist inside you !! So leave all tension behind , get a camping gear , and go on an adventure spree !!

10. Camping

It refers to spending a vacation outside , but instead of booking a luxurious hotel or a affordable lounge , we stay in recreational vehicles or usually set up a tent . Mostly ,the places preferred for camping is woods . or if you want more thrill in your life , then some hilly areas . Camping is much more romantic for couples as you can feel the chilling breeze , do stargazing , spend some quality time etc. It is also good to go camping with friends as you can do many things like lighting a bonfire , barbecue , playing guitar , dancing around , having fun , playing truth and dare , and lots of more stuff . Also it as a plus when you decide to go camping nearby a river bank so that withhand you can do boating , canoeing , fishing , and a lot more.There are lot of places in India to go camping, and here's my recommended list on few of them.

Rishikesh , Uttarakhand
Camping in Rishikesh on the banks of Ganga river is definitely an experience one should have atleast once in their lives. Being very close to nature , you will also feel a spiritual connection . The simple tents have basic facilities which provides a good break from the life in cities. While camping, do not miss river rafting, trekking, yoga, meditation or just sitting along the river banks.
Spiti Valley , Himachal Pradesh
Situated in Keyong district of Himachal Pradesh loaded with a lot of adventurous activities like rappeling, Volley Ball,trekking to Pine forest, river crossing, rock climbing, tug of war, nature walk, sequence climbing and treasure hunt. With untouched hills , lakes , monasteries , valleys , this untouched site has a lot to offer for.
Anjuna Beach , Goa
Sea waves , beach , tents , drinks and nightlife . What else do you want ?.

9. Rafting

Rafting is an outdoor water sports activity in which a group of people use an inflatable raft to navigate through river .The experience is more enjoyable when the water has downstream flow and when you have a large group of friends and then we have kind of competition . This activity is best accompained by resting along side the water bodies accompained withs small talks and barbequed snacks.There are a lot of places to go rafting in India , and the best few recommendations are given.

Rishikesh , Uttarakhand
Rishikesh is the hub of many adventurous and religious activities . No matter what itinerary an adventurer chooses, Rishikesh offers an unforgettable and awe-inspiring rafting experience in the midst of hued mountains and silvery rapids . But , one warning : This rafting tour is fun + danger , because of the speedy flow of water and dangerous rocks along the way.
Zanskar River , Ladakh
With the snow covered mountains and bone chilling water , rafting through the icy cold waters of the Zanskar river is a truly exhilarating experience. This region also coveres some isolated terrains of Ladakh , and also an event named Zanskar river rafting expedition is held every year between July and August , so you can even meet foriegners and camp in groups , while enjoying the beauty of 6000 metre high peaks. But yes , make yourself strong for rejoicing class IV rapids!!
Beas River , Kullu Manali
This rafting stretch in Manali extends for about 20kms and you will get to experience grade 3 and 4 rapids. Best time to travel here is between June and July.

8. Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is an extreme sport in which we need to dive deep into ocean with oxygen cyliners and other supply gears . We get to see the beautiful inner ocean and marine life . Good luck finding pearls in the given listed places !!

Havelock Islands , Andaman and Nicobar Islands
Whats better than a honeymoon vacation on a beautiful island with top class restaurants and cuisines , with a little oomph of adventure for the thrill seeker inside you ? Enjoy scuba diving in many of the similar sights in Andaman and Nicobar Islands alongwith beach parties and candlelight dinners.
Grand Island , Goa
Goa has a lot of contennt for you if you're a party animal or a travel junkie . But as a plus , you will also get to find some of the sites for mini adventures after a few days of chill . Explore beautiful shipwrecks , corals , reefs with other flora and fauna inside the ocean !!
Underwater Delight , Lakshwadeep
One of the best places for scuba diving in India which gives both thrilling and daring experience. There are around 5 beautiful diving spots and you'll get to see vibrant fishes , turtles and a lot more !!.

7. Bungee Jumping

One of the most dangerous yet fun sport .It requires jumping from a great height while being connected from a bungee rope .Its even better if you jump together . Some recommended places for bungee jumping are given below :

MohanChatti , Rishikesh
Rishikesh has the highest bungee jumping spot in India over a platform built over a iron cliff over looking a rocky river HYUL, a tributary of River Ganga. You can also try flying fox here . The length of the jump is 83 meters.
Kunegaon , Lonavala
There is an adventure park named Della's adventures where this bungee jumping takes place . This jump is of 45 meters , and you can also enjoy the 50+ adventurous activities in the park like flying fox , zorbing , mountain cycle and so on.
Anjuna Beach , Goa
Apart from camping , one can also go bungee jumping in Anjuna beach , Goa . With a height of only 25 meters , this is ideal for rookie adventurists and multi-purpose trips.

6. Hang Gliding

Simple , fun and dangerous .It consists of a non motorised vehicle known as a hang-glider , which is flied by the pilot by adjusting his/her bodyweight through the harness he is suspended through. Following are the most recommended places for the sport.

Nandi Hills , Karnataka
A decent place near bangalore for both paragliding and hand-gliding .Situated roughly 1500 meters above the sea level , it offers a true terrifying and exhilarating experience .
Nainital , Uttarakhand
Nainital is a very beautiful place and an emerging spot for hand-gliding . Situated approximately 1200 meters above the sea level , you can feel the clouds passing through you.
Bir , Himachal Pradesh
Bir is a beautiful village located in Himachal Pradesh and is famous for offering a wide array of adveture sports like paragliding , camping and trekking . Best time to visit this place is from October - November and March - June .

5. Paragliding

This is the best sport for rookies who want to try their hands at adventurous sports . After launching from a height , the participant has to glide through the air from a paraglider and change directions using their bodyweight .It is recommended to start your journey with an expert , but once you gain experience you can also do it very well on your own. India has many places for paragliding , and some of the best ones are listed below :

Bir Billing, Himachal Pradesh
Once again , the hub of adventure sports in India , Himachal Prades , is here . With 11 paragliding spots , this place offers the best options.The take-off point is highest in Asia ,approximating about 8000 meters above the sea level .
Manali , Himachal Pradesh
Manali is a mesmerizing beauty located in Himachal Pradesh full of valleys , lakes , rivers , meadows and other beautiful specimens of nature. Now , imagine observing these specs from a height of 6000ft . Exiting , isnt it !!!
Gangtok , Sikkim
Gangtok is famous for sightseeing and is also a famous tourist destination . Paragliding in Gangtok is the best experience one can ever have , as you can see both , the Himalayas , and the landscapes .

4. Canyoning

This sport involves navigating through canyons using wide variety of techiques like abseiling , climbing , sliding , jumping , scrambling , cliff jumping and swimming. The beauty of this sport is it incorporates many different types of extreme sports. This activity is enjoyed the most when it is done in a group. Some sights in India for canyoning are as follows :

Bhrigu Lake , Manali
Apart from a lot of adventure sports,Bhrigu lake is most famous for canyoning . It consists of a 4 day long trip at 14000 meters above the sea level.A lot of tourists come here to experience the thrill.
Cherrapunji , Meghalaya
Cherrapunji is located in about 80kms from Mawsynram , the wettest place on earth . So clearly , you have a plethora of waterfalls and river streams making it a perfect spot for adventurists . There is a large variety in types of canyoning like swim free river canyoning to wet water canyoning.But yes , prepare yourself eith some knives too , as you would be navigating through jungles.
Kolad , Maharashtra
Kolad is one of the best destinations for canyoning in India . The serene atmosphere along the Kundalika Valley of Kolad makes it an excellent canyoning destination . Also , canyoning here is a bit more challenging than other spots because of Kundalika river . Also , you can do other sports here like rafting and tubing.

3. Kayaking

Kayaking involves navigating through water stream using a mini boat called kayak . It is similar to canoeing ,the difference is that in canoeing , there is one sided paddle and we have to kneel and operate it.Though , both are super fun activities. The recommended places to commit these fun activities in India are given below :

Backwaters , Kerela
Kerela is a beuatiful place whose culture , cuisine and religious practices differ greatly from India . Apart from beautiful temples , sceneries , resorts and stays in boathouses , you can also experience kayaking in famous backwaters of Kerela . Offering a scenic and serene vibe , you also get to explore the culture of villages on the way and more.
Nagin Lake , Srinagar
Srinagar is known as paradise on earth and it is known why . But , for some of the thrill seekers , apart from the astonishing beauty of mountains and seas , there are many beautiful lakes and rivers to enjoy kayaking and rafting
Zanskar River , Leh
Leh offers grade I to V rapids for absolute adventure experience . With spine chilling water and many other numerous activities to do alongwith the beauty of mountains and high altitude , this place is great to go !!.

2. Rock Climbing

In this sport the participant has to climb up , down or across a rock formations mostly with the help of special equipments like ropes . The goal is to reach the endpoint , or just to the summit to watch the best sunset of your life. Some places for rock limbing in India are as follows :

Parvati Valley , Himachal Pradesh
With lush greenery , breath taking landscapes and rivers , Parvati Valley is a desirable destination for trekking among travellers , but you can also find desirable terrains for rock climbing expeditions .
Malshej Ghat , Maharashtra
Malshej ghat is a heaven for climbers during monsoons due to its lush green flora and fauna. Some waterfalls , ancient temples and ruins add to the experience !!
Pythal Mala, Kerala
Pythal Mala is perfect for rock climbing , rappelling and other activities . Groups of travellers come to this place to experience the thrilling rock climbing expeditions conducted every year.

1. Sky Diving

Most dangerous sport in this list . You need to jump from a height and open parachute for landing . Extremey risky and comparitively costlier , but worth it . Some places for skydiving in India are as follows :

Mysore, Karnataka
Mysore is best destination for skydiving in India . One full day of ground-training is required to carry out your first solo parachute jump from 4,000ft.Mysore ensures the best views and is a great place to enjoy the safe and professional experience to get the adrenaline rush.
Deesa, Gujarat
Deesa is a gorgeous city close to Ahmadabad in Gujarat. It is one of the most thrilling places for skydiving and has a drop zone which is among top 8 skydiving destinations . Several skydiving camps are hosted here by Gujrat and Indian Parachuting Federation.
Aamby Valley, Maharashtra
Aamby Valley is located close to Pune and is extremely loved by skydiving enthusiasts from different corners of the world . The 10,000 feet jump is a memorable skydiving experience .
